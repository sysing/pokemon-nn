## Key Finding

**Dragon's 100% in v0.1–v0.3 was a sample size illusion.** With only 5 val samples, getting 5/5 had a ~33% chance even at random. With 19 val samples, real accuracy is 89.5% — still good (gold/yellow border is distinctive), but far from perfect.

This is a textbook example of why small validation sets produce unreliable metrics. Always check sample counts alongside accuracy numbers.
